Belarus Paper Money Catalog

Belarus, officially the Republic of Belarus, is a landlocked country in Eastern Europe with Minsk as its capital. It has a diverse landscape with forests, lakes, and marshlands. Belarus has a population of around 9.3 million people and covers an area of approximately 207,600 square kilometers.

The official currency of Belarus is the Belarusian ruble (BYN). Introduced in 1992 after the dissolution of the Soviet Union, the Belarusian ruble has undergone several redenominations due to periods of hyperinflation. The current series, introduced in 2016, replaced the previous one at a rate of 1 new ruble (BYN) for 10,000 old rubles (BYR).

Belarus maintains a largely state-controlled economy with significant industries in machinery, chemicals, and food processing. The National Bank of the Republic of Belarus regulates the country's monetary policy and ensures the stability of the Belarusian ruble. Belarusian monetary policy focuses on controlling inflation, managing exchange rates, and maintaining financial stability.
